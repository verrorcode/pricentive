## Brand Consistency Guidelines

### Color Scheme:
- Primary: Blue (#0066FF or similar)
- Secondary: Purple (#8B5CF6)
- Accent: Green (#10B981) for success states
- Keep consistent with your Tailwind theme

### Image Style:
- Clean, modern, minimal
- Professional enterprise aesthetic
- Consistent lighting and color grading
- Avoid overly busy or cluttered visuals

### Technical Requirements:
- **Format**: PNG for transparency, JPG for photos, SVG for icons
- **Optimization**: Compress images (use TinyPNG, ImageOptim)
- **Next.js**: Serve from `/public/images/` directory
- **Responsive**: Consider 2x resolution for retina displays
